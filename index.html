What CryptoVision is

CryptoVision is an independent educational project designed to help you explore crypto markets with structured, data-driven insights. It combines live market data with technical indicators and AI-inspired analysis so you can understand market context, volatility, and potential scenarios. CryptoVision is not a “signal” service and does not promise profits. Its purpose is to make complex market information easier to read and interpret.

What you can do on CryptoVision

  • Track live prices for major cryptocurrencies using public market data.
  • Explore multi-timeframe views (e.g., 30m, 1h, 4h, 1d) to compare short-term vs long-term behavior.
  • Read a daily market summary that describes overall market “mood” and breadth.
  • Select a coin and view structured AI-style scenarios (bullish/bearish probabilities) based on available inputs.
  • Review news headlines (when enabled) to understand macro context and sentiment drivers.

How it works (data + analysis)

CryptoVision retrieves market information from public sources and may use a backend proxy to improve reliability. The interface then formats raw numbers into readable insights such as percentage moves, market-cap ranking, and basic indicator summaries. Where AI-style predictions are shown, they are presented as informational scenarios and not as guaranteed outcomes. Markets can move unpredictably and no model can forecast with certainty.

Important limitations

  • Educational only: content is provided for informational purposes and should not be treated as financial advice.
  • Not real-time on every request: data may be delayed or cached to avoid rate limits.
  • API availability: if a provider is down or rate-limited, some features may temporarily degrade.
  • No guarantees: predictions, scenarios, and indicator readings can be wrong and must be validated by the user.

Frequently asked questions

Is CryptoVision a trading signal service?

No. CryptoVision provides structured insights and educational scenarios. It does not give buy/sell signals and does not guarantee profits.

Can I rely on the AI prediction as a guarantee?

No. Any AI-style output is informational. Use it to understand possible outcomes and risk ranges, not as a certainty.

Where does the data come from?

CryptoVision uses public market data sources and may route requests through a backend proxy for stability. Exact availability may vary over time.

Why do I sometimes see limits or cooldowns?

Market data providers often enforce rate limits. To keep the app stable, CryptoVision may cache results or enforce short refresh cooldowns.

Does CryptoVision collect personal data?

CryptoVision is designed to be minimal. If analytics or ads are enabled, third-party providers may set cookies as described in the Privacy Policy.
