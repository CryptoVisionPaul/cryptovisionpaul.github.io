AI Crypto Insights

AI-assisted crypto analysis for curious traders and builders.

CryptoVision is an independent project that uses market data, technical indicators and AI-inspired logic to generate structured insights about the cryptocurrency market. It is designed for people who want to better understand trends – not for blind signals or get-rich-quick promises.

Open CryptoVision App

What CryptoVision aims to provide

The long–term goal of CryptoVision is to become a complete toolkit built around:

  • Live prices for major cryptocurrencies
  • Multi-timeframe analysis (30M, 1H, 4H, 1D)
  • Generated insights based on real market data
  • AI-based models for pseudo-signals and trend detection
  • A clean and fast interface that runs directly in the browser

Free vs. future Premium version

CryptoVision is currently available as a free web app, with future plans for:

  • A free tier supported by ads (Google AdSense), focused on informational usage.
  • A premium subscription with more advanced AI models, extra timeframes and ad-free experience.

You can already explore the app in its current prototype form, and later choose whether you want to stay on the free version or upgrade to premium once it becomes available. For an overview of the planned tiers, visit the Premium page.
